<h1 align="center">GRIP</h1>

<p align="center">
  <strong>Track the round you’re actually playing—without losing the story in your scorecard.</strong>
</p>

---

**GRIP** is a focused **golf tracking app** for your phone: plan your bag and courses, play a live round with **clear, one-glance controls**, then dig into **how you really score** over time.

### On the course, it stays out of your way

Large touch targets, a **result-first flow**, and a layout built for **walking and tapping**—not for menus. You log what matters *between shots*, so the app feels like a caddie’s notebook, not a spreadsheet.

### After the round, the numbers start talking

**Play stats** turn finished rounds into insight: **hole outcomes** (from birdies to big numbers), **averages**, **putting trends**, and **handicap-style views** over your history—so you can see *performance*, not just a list of dates.

### What’s different

| | |
| :-- | :-- |
| **In-round first** | Built for active play, not just post-round entry. |
| **Stats that match the game** | Scoring bands, outcome breakdowns, and trends—not generic fitness charts. |
| **Yours, on your device** | Works **offline**; your rounds and bag live locally. |
| **Full-fidelity life** | Back up and merge your data when *you* want—so updates don’t mean starting over. |
